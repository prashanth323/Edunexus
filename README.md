# EduNexus Unified

## What It Is

**EduNexus Unified** is a single portal for running day-to-day school operations: enrolling and tracking learners, coordinating staff, marking attendance, managing fees, supporting teaching and assignments, nurturing admissions enquiries, fleet and hostel oversight, and school-wide announcements. Each person sees what their job requires—nothing more—in a consistent, easy layout.

People sign in securely with email and password. Leaders and headquarters staff working across multiple schools can switch context when permitted; schools with one campus use the same straightforward experience without extra clutter.

The feature set below is grouped into three familiar areas:

| Area | Focus |
|------|--------|
| **SMS** (*School Management System*) | Core administration: people, attendance, money, transport, hostel, communication, and personalised home screens |
| **LMS** (*Learning Management System*) | Subjects, assignments, and shared learning materials |
| **CRM** (*customer relationship management — admissions*) | Turning family enquiries into enrolments through a clear pipeline |

## Feature Guide

---

## SMS — School Management System

### Signing In & Profiles

- Simple **email-and-password login** and sign-out  
- **School context** so users always know which school they’re working in when they belong to—or oversee—more than one  
- A **personal profile** summary (for example name, contact details and how they’re categorized in the system)  

### Home Screens (Dashboards)

The first screen after login is tailored to **who people are**:

- **Head office–style users** — a clear picture across schools available to them (for example how many schools are active) and reminders to pick a working school before opening day-to-day tools  
- **Finance roles** — a finance snapshot on the home screen, plus a shortcut to full fee and payment areas  
- **Principals, school admins, deputy heads** — enrolment strength, staffing, admissions interest, collections, with simple charts where helpful  
- **Admissions and counselling teams** — a view aligned to outreach and enrolment outcomes (complements the CRM pipeline)  
- **Teachers and librarians** — a view centred on sections and responsibilities they manage  
- **Transport supervisors** — a view centred on transport operations  
- **Parents** — a view centred on their children and school touchpoints  
- **Students** — a learner-friendly summary of what matters day to day  

### Students

- A **searchable list of students** with admission details, class and section, basic demographic and enrolment status  
- **Sorting and filtering** so staff can locate learners quickly  
- Allowed staff can **remove** a learner record where school policy permits  

### Staff

- A **directory of school staff**: names, responsibilities, departments, titles, joining dates, and active or leave status  

### Attendance

- **Academic period** resolved automatically (today’s academic year—or the nearest sensible year if none is marked “current”)  
- Browse **sections** active in that year  
- Choose a **date**, see every learner enrolled in that section, and record **presence** as present, absent, late, or half-day in one sweep  
- **Save marks** reliably for the chosen day  

### Finance & Fees

- **Recently recorded payments** for quick reassurance  
- **Month-by-month collections** shown in a chart for leadership and finance conversations  
- Look up learners by admission number or name and open their **unsettled invoices**  
- Browse **recent invoices issued by the school** in one organised list  

### Transport

- **Vehicle register**: registration identifiers, approximate capacity, and whether routes still use each bus  
- **Routes**: names, fares, assignment to buses, active or paused  

### Hostel

- **Room inventory**: numbering, wing or block and floor where you use those ideas, capacity, lodging type, monthly charge, vacant or operational status  

### Notices & Announcements

- A **bulletin-style board** ordered by freshness  
- **Who should see each notice**: everyone, teachers only, learners only, parents only, selected groups—so announcements stay targeted  
- **Priority** (for example urgent vs informational) plus **timing**—when posts went live and when they lapse if applicable  
- Credits to the **publisher** where the system recognises them  

### Settings & Convenience

- Confirm **personal details**, **role label**, and **active school**, alongside switches in the top bar  
- **Light or dark appearance** for comfort across rooms and shifts  
- **Sidebar navigation** grouping every area that person is allowed to open  

### Room to Grow (SMS)

Schedules and timetabling, learner leave requests, detailed fee rules, salaries and school expenditure, finer bus stops and who rides which route, assigning beds to occupants, clearer permission boundaries for sensitive actions, and a stronger trail of who changed what—ready to adopt as your rollout deepens.

## LMS — Learning Management System

Designed for coordinating **what classes study and submit**.

- Overview of **subjects** taught at your school  
- **Assignments**: titles, deadlines, marking weight, aligned subject and level  
- **Shared learning materials**: titles and types—so families and teachers recognise what was published  
- Presented in **clear tabs**—subjects, work due, materials—so nothing important is buried  

### Room to Grow (LMS)

Structured courses with ordered lessons, richer material libraries, collecting submissions from learners, scheduling and running formal assessments, and publishing results back to families—layered on when you are ready.

---

## CRM — Admissions & Enquiries

Treat every family enquiry consistently—from first hello through enrolment—or a polite closure.

- A **pipeline view** arranging prospects by stages (for example newly interested vs already visited vs awaiting decision)  
- Capture **student and guardian names**, contacts, urgency, narrative notes, and **where the enquiry came from**  
- **Move stages** or mark outcomes as the relationship develops  
- Stages run from earliest contact through visits and application milestones to admitted, withdrawn, or not proceeding  

### Room to Grow (CRM)

Formal application packs, visit appointments, counsellor hand-offs, scheduled follow-ups, and deeper reporting on conversion—without replacing the simple pipeline your team uses today.

## Implementation Note (Brief)

Administrators connect the portal to your EduNexus environment and assign each user the right responsibilities so they only see SMS, LMS, and CRM areas meant for their role. Everyday users need only normal familiarity with a web browser.
